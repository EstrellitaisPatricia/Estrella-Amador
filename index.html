Why is Abortion Wrong?

In this video, the narrator explains as to why abortion is wrong and gives out many examples. He states: "If you kill me, you rob me of my future." So when you kill him, he can no longer do the things he wants to accomplish. You are taking away his opportunity to do whatever he desires with his life. It is the same with a fetus. If killing a twenty year old man is considered wrong, worse is killing a fetus. Every man, woman, and child has a future. Who are you to take away this amazing opportunity that is life? Take for example Don Marquis's point of view. The relevant question is: Will the fetus have a Future Like Ours (FLO)? The answer is yes! Every person has their time of difficulties but that is just the human condition. Life is what you make of it and if you kill a baby by having an abortion, you are depriving them of having a future. That is one reason why you shouldn't have an abortion.

Donald Marquis

In this video, the narrator is explaining Don Marquis's argument against abortion. He explains his thesis: Abortion is seriously immoral, except possibly in rare cases. That is technically the argument of a pro-lifer. Now, what would be the argument of a pro-choicer?

Someone who is pro-choice would say that fetuses are not persons OR they are not social beings. Pro-choicers would argument that abortion is totally not wrong and it is NOT killing a human. You may be wondering, what are those rare cases? Now, not everybody will agree with me, I'm sure, when I say that the ONLY cases where abortion SHOULD be an option is when there is a life-threatning situation where either the mother, the child, and/or both are in danger of losing their lives. To me, that seems to be an acceptable decision. That way, the doctor is helping the pregnant woman and her baby. This is the only time where abortion should be llegal. Other than that, it should be illegal.
